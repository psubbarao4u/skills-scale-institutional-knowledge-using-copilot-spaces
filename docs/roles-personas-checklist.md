# Roles and Personas Checklist

This checklist helps teams incorporate all defined personas into project kickoffs and role definitions. Use this during project initiation to ensure clarity on responsibilities and communication channels.

---

## Project Kickoff Checklist

### Core Team Identification
- [ ] Identify and assign **Developers** for the project
- [ ] Assign **Product Manager** for product vision and roadmap ownership
- [ ] Assign **Project Manager** for delivery coordination and risk management
- [ ] Identify **Product Owner** for backlog ownership and sprint prioritization

### Extended Team and Stakeholders
- [ ] Identify key **Stakeholders** who will provide requirements and validate outcomes
- [ ] Identify **Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)** needed for domain or technical expertise
- [ ] Assign **Change Manager** (if applicable) for managing scope or process changes

### Role Clarity and Communication
- [ ] Review and share the [Roles and Personas document](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md) with all team members
- [ ] Confirm each team member understands their responsibilities
- [ ] Establish communication channels for each role (standups, planning, reviews)
- [ ] Document escalation paths and decision-making authority

---

## Role Definition Checklist

Use this checklist when defining or onboarding new roles to a project.

### For Each Role, Confirm:
- [ ] **Role Summary** is documented and understood
- [ ] **Responsibilities** are clearly defined and accepted
- [ ] **Goals** are aligned with project objectives
- [ ] **Communication channels** are established and documented
- [ ] **Interactions with other roles** are clarified (see [Roles and Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md))

---

## Persona-Specific Kickoff Items

### Product Owner
- [ ] Backlog is created and prioritized
- [ ] Acceptance criteria process is agreed upon
- [ ] Sprint planning cadence is established

### Stakeholder
- [ ] Stakeholder list is documented
- [ ] Review and demo schedule is communicated
- [ ] Feedback channels are established

### Subject Matter Expert (SME)
- [ ] SME availability and engagement model is defined
- [ ] Knowledge transfer sessions are scheduled (if needed)
- [ ] Documentation responsibilities are clarified

### Change Manager
- [ ] Change request process is documented
- [ ] Impact assessment template is available
- [ ] Change communication plan is established
